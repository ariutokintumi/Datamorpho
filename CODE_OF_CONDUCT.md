# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Datamorpho is an open protocol project. Everyone who participates — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to engage in a way that is constructive, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct and specific about technical disagreements.
- Assume good faith in others' contributions and questions.
- Keep discussion focused on the protocol, implementation, and documentation.
- Accept that contributions may be declined or revised — that is part of open protocol development.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of others' positions.
- Sustained off-topic disruption of technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Issues of conduct can be reported to g@evvm.org. Reports will be reviewed and addressed at the maintainer's discretion. Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from project spaces.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official Datamorpho communication channel.
